Kabir Bedi Biography

Kabir Bedi is an India film actor. He is well known for his role as Emperor Shah Jahan in Taj Mahal. An Eternal Love Story and the villainous Sanjay Verma in the 80 blockbuster Khoon Bhari Maang.  His career has spanned three continents covering India, the United States and especially Italy  European countries in three media. 

He is best known in Italy and Europe for playing the pirate Sandokan in the popular Italian TV mini-series and for his role as the villainous Gobinda in the 1983 James Bond film Octopussy.

Kabir Bedi has been making the link up headlines for years. He was once in a living relationship with late Parveen Babi and he was married at the time.
