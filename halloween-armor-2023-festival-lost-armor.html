Here is how you can get Destiny 2 Festival of the Lost Halloween Armor 2023 Ornaments for free.

First Published October 18, 2023, 14:39

Destiny 2 Halloween 2023 event, Festival of the Lost, has begun, and players can complete multiple challenges to get a spooky experience in the game and earn a ton of rewards. Apart from this, players are excited about Festival of the Lost Halloween Armor 2023 ornaments, and here is how you can get them.

How To Get Festival of the Lost Halloween Armor 2023 Ornaments In Destiny 2

After the voting, this year's Festival of the Lost Halloween Armor 2023 Ornaments are as follows:

  • Titan: Crawling Chaos Armor (Spider)
  • Hunter: Mygalomorph Armor (Spider)
  • Warlock: Carapace Armor (Beetle)

In order to get them, you will have to go to the Eververse store and purchase them with either Silver or Bright Dust. Each ornament set costs 1,500 Silver or 6,000 Bright Dust, and if you want all three of them, you will have to spend 4,500 Silver or 18,000 Bright Dust, which is a lot, so make sure to grind heavily.
