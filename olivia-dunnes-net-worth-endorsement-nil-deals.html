Olivia Dunne's Endorsement And NIL Deals Have Blown Up Her Net Worth

January 2024 · 6 minute read

Highlights

  • Olivia Dunne's $3.5 million net worth comes from brand deals and NIL earnings.
  • Dunne makes seven figures annually from brand deals.
  • Dunne's success with brand endorsements has far surpassed other female college athletes.

TikTok star and college gymnast Olivia Dunne spends so much time online that fans even wondered if she and boyfriend Paul Skenes met via Instagram. But while her fellow LSU students posted selfies and laughed at reels, Dunne made a fortune from sponsored posts and NIL (Name, Image, and Likeness) deals. According to People, Olivia Dunne has a $3.5 million net worth, and that is thanks to the hard work that she has put into promoting fashion brands and sharing her life as a college athlete. While effort doesn't always translate to a high net worth, as athlete Alicia Schmidt lost money after rejecting a deal, Dunne will likely keep succeeding financially.

Without endorsing brands and NIL deals, it seems unlikely that Olivia Dunne could have earned so much money. After all, most college athletes aren't as wealthy as she is. In this article, we'll look at how Dunne's brand deals increased her net worth, along with the fact that she makes more money than other female college athletes with the NCAA.

How Much Money Does Olivia Dunne Make From Social Media Brand Posts And NIL Deals?

Olivia Dunne earns seven figures annually, according to Parade, from the brand deals that she signs thanks to her popularity as a college athlete. As Parade noted, she tends to represent brands in the fashion space. When interviewed on the podcast Full Send, she explained that she was given over $500,000 for one post on Instagram. While it doesn't seem like that happens all the time, that is definitely an impressive number.

Olivia Dunne grew her net worth after getting her first NIL deal in 2021 and these partnerships are why she has a $3.5 million net worth. According to The Sun, Dunne has been earning money from NIL deals since her first one, which was with Vuroi.

After Vuroi reportedly paid the star six figures, she continued to get many different brand deals. Her autograph and photo were even on a Leaf Trading Card. While the amount that she earned from that is unknown, it must have been lucrative given her social media following. Without deals like these, Dunne wouldn't have a net worth of over $3 million and would likely be a regular college gymnast.

Dunne hopes that others will be inspired by her financial success, and considering that these deals have added to her high net worth, The Livvy Fund is a great idea.

Olivia Dunne Earns More Money Than Other NCAA College Female Athletes

According to People, Dunne is the female athlete with the NCAA who makes the most money. Considering that she has been earning millions since her first NIL deal back in 2021, that isn't surprising, but it is impressive.

While the social media star and athlete said on the Full Send podcast that she doesn't talk about money regularly, Dunne talked about earning so much money from NILs in an interview with The Street. Dunne suggested that other college athletes balance putting away money with having some fun. She said that people should "spend it like a normal college kid" but also invest.

Dunne also gave advice about NILs and said:

"Find a really good agent. That was the first thing I did. And just take your time. Don't rush into anything. Don't be in a rush to make a lot of money."
