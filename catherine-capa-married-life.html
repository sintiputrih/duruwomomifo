Former American Footballer Troy Aikman finally found his soulmate. He walked down the wedding aisle with the love of his life, Catherine 'Capa' Mooty in California after nearly a year-long romantic affair.

Mooty is a fashion retailer in Dallas area where Troy played football in his professional days. He played for Dallas Cowboys where Demarcus Ware also left his mark. Both Capa and Troy are now parents to four children combined from their previous marriage. Want to know more about their big day and their family, then read along.

Troy's Touchdown at the Wedding Venue!

The Hall of Fame quarterback Troy tied the knot with his fiancee in Santa Barbara, California in front of family and friends. They accepted the nuptial vow by stating 'I do' at the same location where Kim Kardashian and her former hubby Kris Humphries were pronounced husband and wife.

Aikman and Capa both kept their wedding a low key from the media. However, there are some pictures which Troy posted on his Instagram page. Former Footballer arrived at the venue of Four Season Hotel in his dashing suit while Catherine looked absolutely stunning in her white strapless satin wedding gown. 

The fashion retailer accepted him as her life-partner in a beach wedding on September 4, 2017, as reported by The Dallas Morning News. And without too much of fuss, the pair traveled back home to Dallas after their big day.

The Romantic Proposal

The duo started dating somewhere around February of 2016. Troy took it slow with his new love before asking the big question.

On June 2, 2017, the couple took a trip to Lake Como, Italy where he romantically proposed Copa. Aikman took his lady love for a boat ride while holding a glasses of wine during their outing. 

In the midst of the outing, Aikman went on one knee and popped up the big question. After Capa said Yes to the former footballer, he made an announcement on his Instagram about his proposal. He expressed about Mooty being the love of his life and the night is one of the best in his life.  

Catherine is a Proud Mother!

From this married relationship with Troy, Capa has not welcomed any child. But from her previous matrimony, Mooty became a proud mother of two sons. Catherine, however, has not provided any insights on her past marital life.

Besides that, Mooty also stays silent on the name of her two children. At the moment, Troy and Mooty both are parenting these two children. Like another footballer Jasmine Plummer, former footballer Troy is also the father of two beautiful daughters. 

Prior to his wedlock with Capa, Aikman was in a marital relationship with Cowboys Publicist Rhonda Worthey. From their conjugal union, Worthey gave birth to Alexa Marie and Jordan Ashley. Rhonda and Troy rang wedding bells in 2001, but after a decade long matrimony, they decided to end their relationship on April 12, 2011.

As of now, Catherine and Aikman are parenting four children. They spend a lot of time together and goes on hiking and traveling to new places. Scrolling through their Instagram, we can see the couple going out on an outing and enjoying their family time.
