Chris Collinsworth, an American football celebrity, is not just a successful NFL National Football League player but also one who succeeded in moving from the playing field to sports media and entrepreneurship. Collinsworth was born on January 27, 1959, in Dayton, Ohio, and rose to prominence as a standout wide receiver for the Cincinnati Bengals during an eight-year career there. Upon retiring, Collinsworth jumped into television broadcasting and excelled as a sports broadcaster commentator, showing off how well he knew this game.

Pro Football Focus, Owned by Chris Collinsworth

Collinsworth owns Pro Football Focus (PFF), a well-known sports site that specializes in thorough analysis, including some related to the NFL and others on NCAA Division I American football. PFF is known for advancements in the development of player grades and a new tiered statistics database that compiles all play-by-play data recorded at both the NFL and FBS levels to build unique analytics tools.
