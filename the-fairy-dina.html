The Fairy Dina

March 2024 · 3 minute read

The Fairy Dina has not yet been featured on the official page of Wikipedia. Dina, called TheFairyDina, came to popularity in the esports community thanks to her esports records on Just Dance’s rhythm game. 

She is a presenter on stage and online since she is a member of the team at one of France’s largest WebTVs, LeStream.fr, where she carries a breath of fresh air and optimistic energies.

The exact information about her age has not been displayed to her fans. However, she wanted her fans to keep on guessing her age by looking at her appearance.

The Fairy Dina: Wiki And Bio

As mentioned above, the official page of Wikipedia has not featured the talented streamer in its account. However, the information about her can be known through her official website called The Fairy Dina. 

She has won the game three times in France and once in Vice-France. She has won a bronze medal and a silver medal at the ESWC’s “All Dance World Cup” in 2017, making her the vice world champion.

​She did not limit herself to Only Dance but also experimented with other dance styles. Notably, in October 2019, she set a Guinness World Record for the highest number of users doing a routine of emotes from a computer game for the giant “Fortnite.”

Dina also shines online every week via her Twitch streams and YouTube posts.
