Why Fans Believe John Mayer And Andy Cohen Are Secretly A Couple

June 2024 · 5 minute read

Highlights

  • Fans speculate that Andy Cohen and John Mayer might be a couple, but Cohen denied any sexual relationship during an interview with Howard Stern.
  • John Mayer's longest public relationship was with Katy Perry, which lasted from 2012 to 2015. They had a duet hit together and dedicated songs to each other.
  • Andy Cohen and Anderson Cooper have a close friendship, but they never dated. They started hanging out together years after their failed blind date and eventually became co-hosts on CNN's New Year's coverage.

So far, 2023 has been a year of odd couples. From Kylie Jenner's "vapid" relationship with Timothée Chalamet to her ex-boyfriend, Tyga's fling with Avril Lavigne. But just when we thought it couldn't get any more random, fans now believe that Andy Cohen and John Mayer are a couple.

The Watch What Happens Live host said that they "are in love with each other." Internet sleuths have also compiled some proof that they might be moving in together... Here's a closer look at their dating histories.

Who Did John Mayer Date The Longest?

Mayer has dated a number of celebrities like Taylor Swift, from whom he had a hard time moving on. The 46-year-old musician was also linked to Kiernan Shipka who's half his age. But his longest public relationship so far was with Katy Perry. They were together, on and off, from 2012 to 2015.

At one point, there were rumors that they'd gotten engaged. In 2013, they released a duet hit, Who You Love. "I've been such a fan, I think he's so incredibly talented and gifted and a genius songwriter," Katy told E! News of their collab (via Who). "So to be a part of anything he was doing was just natural."

They dedicated several more songs to each other, including some breakup ballads. The two reportedly split due to conflicting work schedules. Perry had just embarked on her Prismatic World Tour, and insiders said she knew it would have been hard to juggle that and her romantic life.

In 2017, Mayer said that he hadn't moved on from Perry yet. "Who else would I be thinking about?" he told the New York Times about his then-new song, Still Feel Like Your Man. "And by the way, it's a testament to the fact that I have not dated a lot of people in the last five, six years. That was my only relationship. So it's like, give me this, people."

At that time, the Dark Horse songstress had already been seeing her fiancé, Orlando Bloom. Meanwhile, Mayer had just given up on the celebrity dating app, Raya. "It's just lot of chatter," he said of his experience back then. "We all talk to the same people. There are very few people actually meeting up."

Are Andy Cohen And John Mayer Dating?

Cohen is known to have close friendships with celebrities like Sarah Jessica Parker. It's even their tradition to go to the Met Gala together instead of the Sex and the City star going with her husband, Matthew Broderick. So it's no surprise that Cohen is also that suspiciously close with Mayer.

During a recent interview with Howard Stern, the Bravo star addressed the speculations and swore on his children's lives that he'd never fooled around with Mayer. "I am in love with John Mayer. We are in love with each other," he jokingly explained. Stern then said: "Swear on your children's lives there's never been anything sexual between you and John Mayer," to which Cohen replied, "I swear."

Yes, the Your Body Is a Wonderland hitmaker is straight. Still, imaginative netizens like the idea that the two look like a couple moving in together in some paparazzi photos. In this Instagram clip, someone claimed that they look like they're "buying their dream home together," alongside photos of Cohen's grandmother steaming Mayer's shirt and the pair showing off their matching watches.

Despite many people supporting the wild theory, a commenter argued: "Hate to break this to you…straight men and gay men can be close friends. I'll give you a moment to get the smelling salts and flop properly onto the fainting couch in shock. But let's pretend they were an item… Good. For. Them. They don’t have to tell a soul. Now keep calm and carry on…"

Were Anderson Cooper And Andy Cohen In A Relationship?

Cohen's love life hasn't been as public as Mayer's. However, fans have always wondered if he and fellow TV personality, Anderson Cooper have ever dated. In the early 1990s, the two were set up on a blind date. But Cooper immediately called it off when they spoke on the phone to set up the meeting.

"We had a phone call to set up the date, and I knew within 45 seconds I was never going on a date with Andy Cohen," he shared on The Tonight Show With Jimmy Fallon. "He violated my cardinal rule, which is he asked me about my mom [Gloria Vanderbilt] within the first minute of talking to me."

Cohen then admitted: "I wanted to date the Vanderbilt boy! I was excited!" About a decade later, the two started hanging out with the same circle of friends — vacationing together and eventually developing a solid friendship. In 2015, they kicked off their AC2 tour in Boston. Two years later, they began co-hosting CNN's New Year's coverage.

In 2020, their sons met each other for the first time during a virtual episode of WWHL. Two years later, they had that boozy New Year's coverage that almost got Cohen fired. They remain big supporters of each other despite finding out that they'd hooked up with the same man in 2018.
