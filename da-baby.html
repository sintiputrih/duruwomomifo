Da Baby (born December 22, 1991) is an American Professional Rapper, Recording Artist, and Instagram Star. He was formerly known as Baby Jesus. He started his career in the music industry a few years back. Furthermore, the 27-year-old rapper released his debut album titled “God’s Work Resurrected“. Furthermore, his real birth name is Jonathan Kirk.

His work was published under label South Coast Music Group LLC and distributed by Interscope Records. Moreover, Da gained fame with his single tracks like “Baby Jesus,” “Today,” and “Up The Street”.

In 2017, he released his projects like Billion Dollar Baby, Back on My Baby Jesus Sh!t, and Back On My Baby Jesus $hit. In addition, Baby’s recent work was his album called “Baby Talk” which also gained success.

On July 22, 2018, Da got arrested in Raleigh for possession of a stolen firearm. In fact, the artist also gained a huge controversy when he shot and murdered a 19-year-old Jalyn Domonique Craig.

The incident happened when the rapper was shopping at a Huntersville Walmart on November 5, 2018, and Jalyn attempted to rob him. According to a source, Craig was shot in his abdomen and was declared dead at the scene. Da and along with four people caught by the police at the Huntersville Police department.
