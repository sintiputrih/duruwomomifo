Doja Cat was born on 21st October 1995 in Malibu, California in the United States. She comes from an artistic background. Doja has mixed ethnicity, her father being a South African and her mother Deborah Elizabeth Sawyer is of Jewish and American ethnicity. Doja Cat is in a relationship with her longtime boyfriend Johnny Utah.

Not so much is known about her school life but Doja Cat is said to have studied piano and she loved to dance as a teenager. Career

Dlamini took one of her cat’s names and combined it with her favourite weeds name to come up with a stage name.

Doja released her first song So High on SoundCloud in 2012 and later released Nunchuks in 2013. Both songs received many views pushing her to instant fame. She has over 160K fans on SoundCloud. She was signed with RCA Records later in 2013 together with Kemosabe Records as a joined project but the deal was finalized in 2014. Towards the end of 2014, she released her first EP, Purr. Her song So High was featured in a TV Series Empire soundtrack on the third episode of the first season.

She became more popular and received attention from her fans after she started producing more music on SoundCloud and YouTube. Doja Cat was signed by OGG in 2015. She collaborated with various artists including Elliphant, Escobar and Pregnant Boy. She went on to releases her single Candy in September 2016. The song got attention after it was used on the TikTok app three years later and was charted on the Billboard Hot 100 in December 2019. She has written and produced other songs exclusively on her Soundcloud account.
