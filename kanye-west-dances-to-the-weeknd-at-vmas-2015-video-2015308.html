Kanye West Dances to the Weeknd at VMAs 2015: Video

January 2024 · 1 minute read

Can he get much higher! Kanye West showed his ultimate support for The Weeknd on Sunday, Aug. 30, during the singer’s performance of “Can’t Feel My Face” at the 2015 MTV VMAs.

Like many other celebrities in the crowd, West stood up for the artist during the performance of the 2015 summertime anthem — but surprisingly, the often stone-faced rapper danced! His pregnant-again wife, Kim Kardashian, and his mother-in-law, Kris Jenner, meanwhile, looked up and smiled at West as the rapper swayed his body to the song.

North West’s dad even pointed his fingers in agreement to the beat of the song, especially when the catchy chorus, “I can’t feel my face when I’m with you / But I love it” was played.

That wasn’t West’s only fun moment of the night. Cameras also panned to the rapper grinning with his wife and laughing with his seatmates at one point when Taylor Swift accepted her award for Best Female Video.
