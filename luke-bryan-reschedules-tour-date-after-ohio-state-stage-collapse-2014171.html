Luke Bryan Reschedules Tour Date After Ohio State Stage Collapse

May 2024 Â· 1 minute read

Luke Bryan is doing okay after a scary incident on Jan. 16. Shortly after he performed at Ohio State on Thursday night, a stage collapsed. As a result, the country singer had to reschedule his upcoming show.

"Tonight's Luke Bryan concert in Lexington, Kentucky will be rescheduled for Friday, February 21 due to damage sustained to a piece of the stage during production load out after last night's show in Columbus, Ohio," Bryan's rep tells Us Weekly in a statement. "The damage occurred after the show had ended and no fans were in the building. The tour's team will make the necessary repairs to the production elements so the tour may continue."
