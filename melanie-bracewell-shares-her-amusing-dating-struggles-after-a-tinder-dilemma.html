A single woman has shared her amusing dating struggles after her return to Tinder didn’t go the way she hoped.

Comedian Melanie Bracewell, from New Zealand, uploaded a now-viral TikTok video of her message exchange with a potential date.

The man complimented the 25-year-old on her sense of humour, saying ‘for a woman, you’re pretty funny’ after he ‘super liked’ her on the dating app.

But she never heard from him again after she fired back: ‘Shut the f*** up b****.’
