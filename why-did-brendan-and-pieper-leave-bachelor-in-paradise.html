Bachelor in Paradise is an American reality TV show, which consists of former bachelor stars who have previously been dumped for a second chance to find love!

In the latest episode, Brendan and Pieper made a shock exit from the show, but why?

In this article we will explore what led the couple to leave Bachelor in Paradise, so read below to find out more!

Who are Brendan and Pieper?

Brendan Morais and Pieper James star on the latest season of Bachelor in Paradise.

Brendan is a 31-year-old actor, model, and commercial roofer from Massachusetts.

He formerly appeared on season 16 of The Bachelorette, and joined Bachelor in Paradise for a second shot at love!

Pieper is a 23-year-old model and reality TV star from Happy Valley, Oregon.

She is currently doing a Master’s Degree in Marketing at DePaul University.

Pieper appeared on Matt James’ season of The Bachelor and made it up to week seven before being eliminated.

Why did Brendan and Pieper leave Bachelor in Paradise?

There are lots of rumors circulating across social media as to why the pair left the show.

Although Brendan denies these claims, suggesting that they were ‘casual’ before Bachelor in Paradise, it seems that they started getting exposed and called out for this on the show, so they decided to dip!
