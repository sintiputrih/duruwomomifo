Meet One That Got Away's Kasey Ma! Her Bio, Age, Job, Simon Fung

July 2024 · 5 minute read

Would you jump into a portal that gives you a second chance at love with the one that got away? Well, you definitely would if you were like Kasey Ma who lost her longtime boyfriend of over 7 ½ years.

Keep reading this Kasey Ma Bio to learn all about this The One That Got Away contestant.

One That Got Away: Are Kasey Ma And Simon Fung Still Together?

From the looks of it — No, Kasey Ma and Simon Fung didn’t seem like they were together.

On March 30, 2022, Kasey made an IG post shedding a little light on her dating life. “But in all seriousness, I’m all about taking it slow and casually dating right now before jumping into a full-on exclusive relationship,” Kasey started. She then let her followers know that she was very transparent about her past relationship with every guy she went on dates with.

Finally dropping the bomb, she added, “It’s too much for me now to commit to one guy after being in a 7.5-year relationship.”

Also, her FB relationship status, then, mentioned her to be “single.” All of which hints that Kasey was still searching for “the one.”

For those of you who missed her “search” on The One That Got Away here’s a recall for you. Stepping into the experiment following the end of 7 ½ years of exhausting relationship, Kasey met her first pursuer Dylan, her NYU study buddy, and she knew he was the one she’d she’d walk out with.

However, Simon’s arrival turned the tide!

Simon, another crush from her NYC days, appeared on the show, saying all the right things, and Kasey was more inclined towards him. Though the star and Dylan’s views on family definitely aligned, she preferred Simon for their every value (personal as well as professional) matched to a tee.

Coming from Chinese heritage, their desire for a partner to be optimistic, and their will to always stay career-oriented, it was like they were the same person. So, it was only obvious for Kasey to choose Simon and return to the real world with him.

However, unlike other contestants, she didn’t leave the show with a commitment. Rather, she asked Simon just to “take a shot at this,” and see where the roads took them. And Simon was more than happy to oblige as well.

Did you know: Besides sharing 7 ½ years of relationship with her ex, Kasey also shared two dogs and a car with him.

Kasey Ma Age

Kasey Ma was 30 years of age when she appeared on The One That Got Away in 2022.

She does look a lot younger though.

Kasey Ma Job

Kasey Ma was working as a full-time Fashion/Lifestyle Blogger and Influencer at her company TheStyleWright LLC when she appeared on The One That Got Away in 2022. She founded the company back in July 2015, publishing writings/YouTube videos about personal style, current fashion trends, and lifestyle inspirations.

Now, TheStyleWright LLC offers services like Creative Strategy, Creating/Planning Overall Event Timeline, Budget/Inventory Management, Oversee Set-up And Breakdown, and Maintain Communication For Overall Efficiency.

Besides running her company, Kasey also is a Twitch Partner. She streamed 3 days a week and had built a very loyal and strong audience while she played games.

Thanks to her online popularity, she was also earning quite well selling her merch.

But if we were to go all the way to the start of her career, Kasey started as a personal marketing assistant at MetLife in 2006. Since then, she’s worked for companies including YMCA, American Red Cross of Northern New Jersey, New York Knicks, Abercrombie & Fitch, Stella McCartney, Jussara Lee, Ideeli, Young Hot & Modern, Aeropostale, NYU CAS Entrepreneurship Association, South Street Securities LLC, Lord & Taylor, Macy’s, and Antuit.

Also, the star has spent some time volunteering for God’s Love We Deliver, St. Francis Food Pantries and Shelters, and St. Jude Children’s Research Hospital – ALSAC.

Trivia: Kasey graced an episode of Good Morning America in June 2019.

Kasey Ma Family

Kasey Ma comes from a strict family of four.

Sadly, her mother Oi-Yim Helen Ma passed away on April 24, 2021, at the age of 61.

The star missed her dearly and on the first mother’s day without her mother, Kasey took it to her IG to share memories of them captioned, “’ll always be sad that I couldn’t see you right before you took your last breaths, but I was right there after you did. Maybe you were happy with my goodbye the night before.”

As for Kasey’s father, Chin-Hung Terence Ma was alive and doing well before 2022.

Talking about her siblings, Kasey has a brother named Terence Ma. He was a vocal coach who owned The SPAA (Singing and Performance Arts Athletics).
