Gwen Stefani Asks A Mega Celebrity To Be Her Maid Of Honor

April 2024 · 1 minute read

In mid-March 2021, Gwen Stefani sat down for a virtual one-on-one with Ellen DeGeneres. During the interview, DeGeneres asked Stefani about her wedding plans, and offered her assistance, according to People. Stefani didn't skip a beat, and even asked DeGeneres to be in the wedding! "I was thinking about that a lot, and I was picturing like a mauve bridesmaid. Maybe maid of honor, you know? We can put some extensions in, and we can make it a full flower arrangement. All that stuff," Stefani joked. 

DeGeneres accepted the offer, letting Stefani know that she'd be there in any way that she could. Oh, and the hair extensions? No problem. "I don't mind doing any of that for you, because you're my friend and anything you ask, I will wear. I will put extensions in my hair. Anything at all. That's how much I love you Gwen," the host said.

While DeGeneres probably won't actually be the maid of honor at Shelton and Stefani's event, she will more than likely be invited — if the COVID-19 restrictions are lifted by that time. She is very good friends with both of the entertainers.
