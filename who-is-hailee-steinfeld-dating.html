Hailee Steinfeld unveils latest romance with quarterback Josh Allen: Everything to know here!  

Known for her stellar acting and singing career, Hailiee Steinfeld is one of the most prominent young Hollywood A-listers. She first landed her breakthrough as a 14-year-old in the 2010 film, True Gift, for which she earned a BAFTA award and an Oscar nomination.  

Steinfeld grew her onscreen presence with roles in Ten Thousand Saints, Romeo & Juliet, Ender's Game, and more recently, Bumblebee, Hawkeye, and Dickinson, in which she portrayed American poet Emily Dickinson. Steinfeld is one of the few child stars who've gracefully transitioned into an A-lister without any career-derailing scandals or moments of notoriety.  

Who is Hailee Steinfeld Dating?

Steinfeld and Allen jetted off for a romantic trip to Mexico, where the duo packed heavy on PDAs. The steamy makeout session comes a month after the songstress and her new beau were pictured on a stroll in NYC. Dishing on the duo's new romance, an insider said, "It's new and they are having fun."   

While fans await more deets on the twosome's budding relationship, here's everything to know about Allen. Josh has been playing quarterback position for the Buffalo Bills of the NFL since 2018. Steinfeld and Allen's many PDA-packed appearances are enough to set off the new couple alert!

Dive into Hailee Steinfeld's Romantic History!  

Steinfeld has navigated a high-profile professional journey and dealt with the constant scrutiny with grace. The Oscar-nominated actress surely has experienced her share of romances in an industry filled with attractive men, but the Let Me Go hitmaker isn't known to be the most forthcoming star when it comes to her love life.   

In fact, throughout her life in the limelight, which spans from her early teenage years to now, Steinfeld has only ever sparked a couple of dating headlines. Hailee's first public relationship was with Instagram influencer Cameron Smoller, with whom she was first linked in 2016.  

The Edge of Seventeen actress even made her red-carpet debut with Smoller at a Golden Globes afterparty, in 2017. Aside from their several public appearances, Hailee and Cameron would often appear on each other's Instagram. But what was thought to be a beginning of an adorable romance turned out to be a fleeting experience.   

Steinfeld and Smoller reportedly called it quits in late 2017. But for what it's worth, the pair seems to have maintained a friendly relationship. The two were spotted enjoying a reunion at a Billie Eilish concert in 2019. Hailee's most notable romance still remains a mystery.  

She was linked with former One Direction singer, Niall Horan, in 2018. The rumored couple was first spotted watching Hamilton at London's West End, in mid-2018. Amid the brewing romance rumors, Steinfeld and the Slow Hands crooner gave the speculation a green light as they packed on PDA in LA, in August 2018.  

But despite the rising interest in the then-new love birds' love story, Steinfeld and Horn gave fans little to nothing. The duo's romance reportedly fizzled out in December of the same year. The Starving hitmaker is believed to have alluded to her budding romance with Nial in a Cosmopolitan interview, in 2018.  

"When you fall in love, you pick up on the tiniest things about yourself," Hailee said before adding, "I am one to spread out on the mattress and take up every inch of space." The Dickinson actress also explained the high walls around her personal life.  

"I am a very private person. I do love that I have the opportunity to share with them what I’m comfortable with and they're there to listen and support me," said the Oscar-nominated actress. Aside from the aforementioned relationships, Hailee was also linked with Charlie Puth and Justin Bieber but the rumors were short-lived due to lack of substantial proof.
