Tara Palmer-Tomkinson Parents: Who Is Charles Palmer-Tomkinson And Patricia Palmer-Tomkinson?

August 2024 · 2 minute read

I’m A Celebrity Get Me Out of Here star, Tara Palmer Tomkinson was born to her parents Patricia and Charles Palmer-Tomkinson.

Patricia Palmer-Tomkinson

Tara’s mum, Patricia, is an ex-banker, who worked at Rothschilds bank, in London.

She was formerly Patricia Dawson before she got married to Charles Palmer Tomkinson

Patricia hails from Argentinaband attended the Sherborne School for Girls in Dorset.

Charles Palmer-Tomkinson

Tara’s father, Charles is a former skier who used to represent Britain in international competitions.

He had represented his country at the 1964 Winter Olympics.

Charles is a landowner, Philanthropist and a close friend of King Charles.

Who Was Tara Palmer-Tomkinson?

Tara Palmer-Tomkinson was a British socialite and TV personality who made appearances in many TV shows, including I’m a Celebrity…Get Me Out of Here!.

Tara co-hosted the programme Animals Do the Funniest Things, together with Tony Blackburn.

She also presented many shows and programmes including The British Comedy Awards…Party On, What Kids Really Think, Popworld, Top of the Pops, SMTV Live, Company Magazine Bachelor of the Year, Dumb Britain, Extreme, The Hits and the Living TV programme Dirty Cows.

Tara took part in the BBC’s Comic Relief Does Fame Academy in 2007.

Tara doubled as an author while she was still alive, having written a number of novels such as The Naughty Girl’s Guide to Life, Inheritance, and Infidelity.

Tara passed away on February 8, 2017, after suffering from perforated ulcer. She was aged 45.

Tara had earlier been diagnosed with a pituitary tumour and an autoimmune condition, before her demise.

The late celebrity was buried on February 27, 2017.
