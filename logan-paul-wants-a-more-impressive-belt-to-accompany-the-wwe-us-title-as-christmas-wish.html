Logan Paul Wants A More Impressive Belt to Accompany the WWE US Title as Christmas Wish

May 2024 · 2 minute read

Logan Paul became one of WWE’s biggest surprises as his athletic ability helped him excel at a level no one thought was possible. He is now the United States Champion and clearly has a target on his back. That said, he has an interesting Christmas wish as well.

Logan Paul has had a lot of fun as the US Champion so far. He took shower while wearing his title and this caused a lot of controversy, but a lot of amusing reactions to boot. Clearly, things have been going well for him so far.

The Holiday season is upon us and WWE Superstars have already gotten into the Christmas spirit. The Maverick took to his Instagram and uploaded a photo of himself and his fiancee, as he wore a Santa outfit while also sporting the US Title.

The Maverick also made it clear he wants just one thing for Christmas – a bigger belt, more so than the United States Championship he is currently in possession of as well.

”all i want for christmas is a bigger belt’‘

In the United States Championship Tournament, Kevin Owens will be duking it out with Santos Escobar in the finals to decide the number one contender for Logan’s title. It remains to be seen who will end up challenging Paul for the title.
