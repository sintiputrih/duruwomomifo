10. Is Teyana Taylor Eazy-E's daughter?

In 2016, a rumour circulated that rapper Eazy-E was the father of Teyana Taylor. The confusion was a result of Eazy-E’s daughter, Erin, appearing on MTV's 'My Super Sweet 16' - the same series Taylor appeared on. Both episodes aired in February 2007 but, despite the confusion, the rumours are false. Picture: Getty/Ruthless Records
