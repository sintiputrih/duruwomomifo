Nathan Lane | Career

Although he has numerous appearances in TV shows and films, Nathan Lane’s career has highly revolved around Broadway.

In the 90s, Nathan appeared in a number of different successful Broadway series such as ‘On Borrowed Time,’ ‘Guys and Girls,’ and more.

For his performance in ‘On Borrowed Time,’ he received his first of many Tony Awards nominations.

Similarly, in 1999, he would go on to voice Snowbell in the film Stuart Little.

And then, in 2001, his appearance in ‘The Producers’ earned him another Tony Award.

He would also earn a nomination for the Golden Globe Award for Best Actor for the musical film adaptation of the play.

Similarly, Lane has had minor to recurring roles in the TV series ‘Modern Family,’ ‘The People vs. O. J. Simpson’ and ‘The Good Wife,’ and more.

For the voice acting part of his career, he has lent his voice to two Disney series, ‘Timon and Pumba’ and ‘Teacher’s Pet.’

His work on Timon and Pumba has earned him two day time Emmys.
