Who is Jim Caviezel?, Jim Caviezel Biography and Wiki

Jim Caviezel is an American actor, he portrayed Jesus Christ in Mel Gibson’s The Passion of the Christ (2004) and starred as John Reese on the CBS science-fiction crime drama series Person of Interest (2011-2016). Caviezel was born on September 26, 1968, in Mount Vernon, Washington, the United States as James Patrick Caviezel. His other notable roles include Private Witt in The Thin Red Line (1998), Detective John Sullivan in Frequency (2000), as well as Edmond Dantès in The Count of Monte Cristo (2002).
